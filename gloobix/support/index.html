Gloobix.io Support

We're here to help you get the most out of Gloobix.io. If you're experiencing any issues, please check the information below. If you can't find a solution, don't hesitate to contact us.

Contact Us

You can reach our support team through the following channels:

  • Email: support@gloobix.io
  • Discord: Join our Discord server

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

I can't log in.

Make sure you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it here.

I'm experiencing connection issues.

Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, it might be a server issue on our end. Please check our social media channels for updates.

I'm having problems with my skins.

Ensure that the skin is properly equipped in your profile settings. If you're still facing issues, clear your browser cache or try a different browser.

Thanks for playing Gloobix.io! We hope you continue to enjoy the game.
