Introduction

Gloobix.io is an online strategy game where players must build and manage a base to steal from other players.

Development

Gloobix.io has several unique features that make it different from other games, such as the ability to build bases in various locations, the option to migrate to new bases and integration with networks.

Migraciones

Gloobix.io allows players to migrate to a new base after completing a level or when reaching a specific objective.

Networks

Gloobix.io has the ability to connect with other players through networks and allow the exchange of resources and strategies.
